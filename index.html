About blockEDU

blockEDU is a system of DAUs (Decentralized Autonomous Universities) enforced by applied cryptographic properties deployed in blockchains like Bitcoin and Ethereum. The goal or consensus the protocol tries to achieve is to provide quality education that does not belong to only those that can afford it. We have built a protocol on distributed systems that enforces this consensus.
